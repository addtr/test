7300 Pearl St — Bethesda, MD

This investment or owner-user property is located in Bethesda, Maryland — one of the DC Metro's most sought-after commercial markets. Bethesda features a dense, affluent daytime and residential population, proximity to the NIH campus, and strong retail and office demand driven by major employers in the healthcare, legal, and government contracting sectors.

Pearl Street runs through Bethesda's established commercial core, offering excellent connectivity to Wisconsin Avenue, Old Georgetown Road, and the Bethesda Metro station on the Red Line. Properties in this location benefit from high foot traffic and the area's continued commercial investment.

This is an exceptional opportunity for an investor or owner-user seeking a presence in one of suburban Maryland's premier commercial districts. Contact JPRES for pricing, square footage, and additional details.
