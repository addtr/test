Property Management

JPRES manages over 150,000 square feet of office and mixed-use properties across Washington, DC and Northern Virginia — maintaining 100% occupancy through proactive management and strong tenant relationships.

Full-Service Management

Managing Properties Like Our Own

JPRES approaches property management the way a hands-on owner would — with close attention to building condition, tenant relationships, and operational efficiency. As a boutique firm, our clients receive direct access to principals, not call centers or rotating staff.

Our management portfolio includes office buildings and mixed-use properties in Washington, DC and Northern Virginia. We maintain long-term occupancy through proactive tenant communication, preventive maintenance programs, and competitive market positioning.

  • Day-to-day operations and facilities coordination
  • Tenant communication and relationship management
  • Rent collection and financial reporting
  • Vendor procurement and contract oversight
  • Preventive and corrective maintenance programs
  • Lease administration and renewal negotiations
  • Capital improvement planning and execution
  • Owner reporting and asset performance analysis

What's Included

Comprehensive Management Services

Financial Management

Rent collection, accounts payable and receivable, monthly financial reporting, annual budgeting, and CAM reconciliation. Owners receive clear, timely statements.

Tenant Relations

Responsive communication, maintenance request handling, lease compliance, and renewal negotiations. We keep tenants satisfied — reducing turnover and vacancy.

Building Operations

Vendor oversight, preventive maintenance scheduling, emergency response coordination, and capital improvement planning to preserve and enhance asset value.

Leasing Integration

Because we are also a leasing brokerage, managed properties benefit from in-house marketing and tenant procurement when vacancies arise — minimizing downtime.

Compliance & Risk

Building code compliance, certificate of occupancy management, insurance coordination, and contractor licensing verification.

Owner Reporting

Monthly and annual reporting packages including income statements, rent rolls, occupancy summaries, and capital expenditure tracking — accessible online.
