Our Story

A Family Legacy in DC Real Estate

Founded in 1980, JPRES has built a reputation on integrity, deep market knowledge, and relationships that span generations.

Our Founding

Built on Four Decades of Expertise

Jackson Prentice Real Estate Services, LLC (JPRES) was founded in 1980 by Jackson B. Prentice and his wife, Mary Ruth Prentice. Born in Washington, DC in 1949, Mr. Prentice has either lived or worked in the city his entire life — accumulating an unmatched understanding of the region's commercial real estate landscape.

What began as a focused brokerage serving the DC Metro area has grown into a full-service commercial real estate firm with expertise spanning land assemblage, investment sales, leasing, and property management across the District of Columbia, Maryland, and Northern Virginia.

JPRES operates with the values of a family business: accountability, long-term relationships, and a personal investment in every transaction. Clients work directly with principals — not junior associates — from start to finish.

The People Behind JPRES

Meet the Team

Jackson B. Prentice

Founder & Principal

Jackson B. Prentice has been active in the Washington, DC commercial real estate market for over four decades. Born and raised in the District, he brings an intimate understanding of the city's neighborhoods, regulatory environment, and evolving commercial corridors that few brokers can match.

Mr. Prentice has participated in landmark land assemblages, complex investment sales, and long-term leasing assignments that have shaped major DC neighborhoods. His relationships with developers, institutions, and property owners span multiple generations of DC's real estate community.

Licensed: DC · Maryland · Virginia

Hannah Prentice Traul

Principal

Hannah Prentice Traul joined JPRES in 2004, bringing a rigorous academic foundation to the firm's brokerage and management practice. She holds a Master's degree in Real Estate from the Carey Business School at Johns Hopkins University, one of the nation's leading real estate graduate programs.

Hannah focuses on leasing, investment sales, and property management — serving both institutional and individual clients across the DC Metro area. Her analytical approach and deep client relationships have made her an indispensable part of the JPRES team.

MS Real Estate, Johns Hopkins University · Licensed: DC · Maryland · Virginia
