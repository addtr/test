Service 01

Land Assemblage

Land assemblage — the acquisition of multiple contiguous parcels to create a larger development site — requires deep market expertise, discretion, and the patience to negotiate with multiple owners simultaneously. JPRES has participated in countless hard-to-acquire land purchases for strategic assemblages over the past several decades.

Our track record includes assemblages in Washington, DC neighborhoods ranging from Capitol Hill to Anacostia, as well as inner-ring suburban markets in Virginia and Maryland. We work with developers, institutional investors, and private landowners to structure complex multi-parcel transactions.

  • Off-market parcel identification and outreach
  • Multi-owner negotiation and coordination
  • Highest and best use analysis
  • Zoning and entitlement guidance
  • Joint venture and disposition structuring

Why Land Assemblage Requires a Specialist

Assembling contiguous parcels demands confidentiality, patience, and simultaneous negotiation with multiple parties — each with different motivations. JPRES's decades of relationships across DC enable us to navigate these deals where others cannot.

Representative Markets

Capitol Hill · Anacostia · NoMa · Columbia Heights · Shaw · Alexandria · Arlington · Bethesda

What Is Investment Real Estate?

Investment properties — office buildings, retail centers, mixed-use developments, and multifamily assets — are valued based on income and upside potential. Determining the highest and best use requires both analytical rigor and local market intuition.

Typical Transaction Types

Owner-user acquisitions · Value-add office · Ground-lease structures · 1031 exchange dispositions · Portfolio sales

Service 02

Investment Sales

JPRES assists a wide variety of owners and buyers in investment transactions, from single-asset acquisitions to multi-property portfolios. Our principal-level involvement means every engagement receives experienced analysis and hands-on deal management.

We help clients identify, evaluate, and execute on opportunities across office, retail, and mixed-use property types — with particular expertise in the mid-market transactions that institutional brokerages often overlook.

  • Buyer representation and property sourcing
  • Seller representation and marketing
  • Valuation and market analysis
  • Due diligence coordination
  • Off-market deal origination
Service 03

Leasing

JPRES proactively represents both landlord and tenant clients in their commercial leasing needs. Whether you're a property owner seeking to stabilize occupancy or a business searching for the right space, we bring market intelligence and negotiating experience to every lease.

Our leasing expertise spans office, retail, and mixed-use properties across Washington, DC and the surrounding metro area. We take a long-term view — structuring leases that work for both parties and support lasting occupancy.

  • Landlord representation and tenant procurement
  • Tenant representation and space search
  • Market rental rate analysis
  • Lease negotiation and deal structuring
  • Renewal and expansion negotiations
  • Build-out and tenant improvement coordination
Discuss Your Leasing Needs

Landlord Representation

Tenant Representation

We represent tenants at no cost, helping businesses find the right space, negotiate favorable terms, and avoid common pitfalls in commercial leases.
