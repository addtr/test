Our Story

A Family Legacy in DC Real Estate

Founded in 1980, Jackson Prentice Real Estate Services, LLC (JPRES) is a family-owned company specializing in commercial and residential real estate in the greater Washington, DC metropolitan area.

JPRES was founded by Jackson B. Prentice and his wife, Mary Ruth Prentice. Mr. Prentice was born in Washington, DC in 1949 and has either lived or worked in DC all of his life, carrying deep knowledge of the city's evolving commercial landscape.

Hannah Prentice Traul, their daughter, joined JPRES in 2004 and holds a Master's in Real Estate from Johns Hopkins University. Together, they offer a rare combination of long-standing relationships, market depth, and personalized service.
